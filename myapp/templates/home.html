The Story Behind Our
Family Reunion

Our family reunion is a celebration of unity, heritage, and the strong bonds within our Ezhava community. Inspired by Sree Narayana Guru, who led the spiritual and social awakening of our caste, we gather to honor his teachings of equality, love, and togetherness. Rooted in Kollam, Kerala, this reunion brings our family closer — across generations and distances — to cherish our shared legacy and create lasting memories.
